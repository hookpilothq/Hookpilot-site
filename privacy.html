HookPilot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-25

HookPilot (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides content planning and performance optimization tools for TikTok creators and businesses.

1) Data We Collect

  • Account information (email, encrypted password, optional name)
  • User content (drafts, hooks, captions, uploaded footage)
  • Performance data (views, watch time, completion, shares, saves, comments)
  • Connected platform metadata and tokens when integrations are enabled

2) How We Use Data

  • Provide product features
  • Improve recommendations and analytics insights
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse

3) TikTok Data Usage

If you connect TikTok, we process authorized TikTok data only to provide analytics and optimization features. We do not sell TikTok user data.

4) Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only share with service providers required to operate the service.

5) Retention & Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support@hookpilot.app.

6) Contact

Email: support@hookpilot.app
