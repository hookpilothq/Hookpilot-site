HookPilot Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-25

HookPilot (“we”, “us”, “our”) helps creators and businesses generate content drafts and improve short-form video performance. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1) Data We Collect

  • Account data (email, encrypted password, optional name)
  • Content data you create in the app (drafts, hooks, captions, settings)
  • Performance data you provide or connect (views, watch time, completion rate, shares, saves, comments)
  • Connected platform metadata (e.g., TikTok account handle and auth tokens if connected)

2) How We Use Data

  • Provide app features (draft generation, video processing, exports)
  • Improve recommendations and performance insights
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and support users

3) Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only share data with service providers needed to run the product (hosting, billing, analytics), under appropriate agreements.

4) Retention

We retain account/content data while your account is active, and for a limited period after deletion for legal/security reasons.

5) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No system is 100% secure.

6) Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.

7) Third-Party Platforms

If you connect TikTok or another platform, your use is also subject to that platform’s terms and policies.

8) Contact

Contact: support@hookpilot.app (replace with your real support email before launch).
